Principle Message

RIDHUN DEV KM,
PRINCIPAL

Welcome to another exciting year, 2019 at Government Industrial Training Institute, Kozhikode, Kerala.

The main goal of this institute is to provide skilled man power to various Industries and to provide employment to the youth. Keeping this goal in mind we impart training to the trainees in different trades as per the guidelines of Director General of Employment and Training, New Delhi. Domestic and global employment opportunities in various fields are increasing day by day which can be availed by the skilled and multi skilled trainees passing out from this Institute.

Thousands of students have benefited from this institution and are spread all over the globe, holding decent positions in various establishments and many of them have started their own business and are successful entrepreneurs.

As a Principal, I will do my best to maintain the training standards, discipline and other essentials and at the same time, introduce new ideas and programs. The worlds around us are changing but that does not mean we have to abandon old values that have helped create the wonderful environment that is established at Gvot. ITI Kozhikode.

All this cannot be done by just one person; it is the culmination of quality work by trainees, staff and parents. So come, join us through the various training programs and upgrade yourself. We offer and assure you the best training in this institute.

Thank you for taking the time to read this message. I look forward to meeting each and every one of you as the year progresses. Please do not hesitate to contact me at any time. Have a great year.


Warm Regards,

RIDHUN DEV KM,
PRINCIPAL
